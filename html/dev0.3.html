4.GEARS - You will be able to equip them on your brawlers

Rare gears:
-HP Regen gear: Regenerates the user's HP every turn (3% of the max HP)
-MP Regen gear: Regenerates the user's MP every turn (3% of the max MP)

Super rare gears:
-Mana gear: Increases the user's maximum amount od MP by 20%
-Health gear: Increases the user's maximum amount of HP by 10%
-Shield gear: Increases the user's DEFENSE stat by 15%
-Speed gear: Increases the user's AGILITY stat by 15%
-Damage gear: Increases the user's ATTACK stat by 15%

Epic gears:
-Super Charge gear: The user's TP charge rate increases by 15%
-Resistance gear: Makes the user resistant to Poison, Paralysis or Stun states.
-Vision gear: The user's critical hit rate increases by 15%
-Evasion gear: The user's evasion rate increases by 10%

Mythic gears:
-Experience gear: The user will gain double the XP
-Coin gear: The party will get double the Coins
-Utility gear: Speed, Shield and Damage gear combined

Legendary gears:
-Repeat gear: A 20% chance to be able to act a second time (10% to get a 3rd action, 5% for 4th and so on)
