5.BALANCE CHANGES

-The addition of hypercharges: Hypercharges are the final abilities for the brawler learned at lvl 40. They include using their super, boosting all their stats and lasting 4 turns. Activating a hypercharge nullifies all the other stat buffs earned during the battle though. They will cost 100 TP, so normal supers will now cost 50 TP. The TP gain from various skills is reduced in half because of that.

-Stat buffs and nerfs: All the stat buffs and nerfs will now be the same (kinda like in pokemon) and all of them either increase or decrease the stat value by 25%, no more no less. And they last five turns.

El Primo:
-Flying Elbow Drop and El Fuego: a.atk * 12 - b.def * 2 --> a.atk * 15 - b.def * 2
-Meteor Rush: Now it deals damage as he always should have been, but boosts only his own speed now, AGI +20% --> AGI +25%
-Always Smiling: DEF +100% for 3 turns --> DEF +25% for 5 turns
-Suplex Supplement: Stun and -50% DEF for 3 turns --> Stun for 1 to 3 turns and -25% DEF for 5 turns
-Meteor Strike: a.atk * 16 - b.def * 2 --> a.atk * 18 - b.def * 2
-Hypercharge added: Gravity Leap - Learned on lvl40 (max) - El Primo damages all the enemies, burns and stuns them! Boosts his speed by 50%. Works for 4 turns. (basically 2 star powers at once)

Buster:
-Busty ???: ATK -20% --> ATK -25%
-Slo-mo Replay: AGI -10% --> AGI -25%

Doug:
-Fast Food: AGI +15% --> AGI +25%
-Self Service: Now when bought, deletes the Snack Attack skill, Doug heals by 1000HP after using self service.
-Expired Food: a.atk * 25 - b.def * 2
-Cold Dog: a.atk * 25 - b.def * 2
