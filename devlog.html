5.BALANCE CHANGES

-The addition of hypercharges: Hypercharges are the final abilities for the brawler learned at lvl 40. They include using their super, boosting all their stats and lasting 4 turns. Activating a hypercharge nullifies all the other stat buffs earned during the battle though. They will cost 100 TP, so normal supers will now cost 50 TP. The TP gain from various skills is reduced in half because of that.

-Stat buffs and nerfs: All the stat buffs and nerfs will now be the same (kinda like in pokemon) and all of them either increase or decrease the stat value by 25%, no more no less. And they last five turns.

ALPHA 0.2 - 31.10.2023

1.LEVELLING REWORK

I've come up to a conclusion that keeping the same exact level system as in brawl stars makes no sense in an RPG Maker game and it won't be fun at all.

Here's how it works now:
-Brawlers will have 40 levels.
-Because of 40 levels instead of 11, the scaling will match the one from before the September 2023 update. In other words, the numbers, at least at the max level
will be the same as before the hypercharge update, but the values are spread across 40 levels instead of 11.
-Level scaling: 5% --> 1,25%

Take a closer look to understand what I mean:

El Primo:

lvl1-6075HP
lvl2-6150HP
lvl3-6225HP
lvl4-6300HP

and so on... +75HP each level, which is 1,25% scaling instead of previous 5%. (0,0125 * 6000 = 75)

lvl40-9000HP

The power 11 HP stays untouched, just as it was before the update.

lvl1-0MP
lvl40-500MP

(usually it's around +13 per level, all brawlers have the same MP amount)
